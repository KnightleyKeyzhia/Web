Player Unknown's Battle Ground (PUBG)

PUBG was created by Brendan Greene from Ireland, in which this is a Battle Royale game. The goal of PUBG is to be the last player to survive from 100 other players in a designated zone.


Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends is a game created by Moonton developer. Players work together to launch strategies to destroy your opponent's towers. Mobile Legends can be played on Android and iOS platforms.


The SIMS

The Sims is a simulation game of daily activities. Players can choose their character and play like real life like eating, sleeping, working, and communicating with other characters.


DOTA 2

DOTA 2 is played by 2 teams with 5 members. Each team has an Ancient that the opposing team must destroy in order to win the game.
